EpicWorship 2 ElectronJS Tech Demo

The purpose of this simple tech demo is to show the current progress of migrating the EpicWorship Project from old, slow Java/JavaFX to lightning-fast ElectronJS!

EpicWorship 2 will be lighter, faster, and much more flexible than Version 1 thanks to it being fully built using 100% HTML5/CSS3/Javascript.

Projectors!

With EpicWorship 2 You will also be able to launch as many projectors as you want and you can project to one or all of them. E.g. I launched a couple for you already (Auditorium and Hallway). Which do you want to project to?
